# Editorial Focus — Prompt Response ICS

## What this is
Industry news feed for Insta360 ICS (creative studio) employees. Competitive intelligence, creative inspiration, and production knowledge — in bilingual English/Chinese format.

## Priority companies
- **DJI** — product launches, strategy, drone regulations, new camera tech
- **GoPro** — product news, strategy shifts, creator ecosystem
- **Apple** — Vision Pro, spatial video, iPhone camera updates, platform policy
- **Nothing** — phone/camera tech, brand strategy, design approach

## Priority topics
- Social media creator campaigns worth studying
- YouTube trends and platform shifts
- Emerging visual formats (spatial, 360, vertical, immersive)
- Production techniques and workflow innovations
- Brand-creator partnerships and UGC strategies

## Categories
- `visual_trends` — shooting styles, format shifts, spatial video, immersive content
- `campaigns` — brand campaigns, UGC strategies, influencer activations
- `production` — gear techniques, editing workflows, color/post, new tools
- `creator_study` — specific creators doing something new, platform strategy

## Context for editorial decisions
This is for creative professionals at a camera/imaging company. They care about:
- What competitors are doing (DJI, GoPro, Apple, Nothing)
- How creators actually use cameras and content tools
- What visual trends are emerging on social platforms
- Production techniques that push the craft forward

## Avoid
- Pure AI model news (covered by the separate Prompt Response finder)
- Crypto/blockchain
- Enterprise SaaS (unless it directly affects creative workflows)
- Generic "top 10 cameras" listicles
- Press releases restated without angle
